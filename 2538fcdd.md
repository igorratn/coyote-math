In the half-plane $x > 1$, the steady transverse displacement $u(x,y)$ of a non-uniform membrane satisfies
$x^2 u_{xx} + 2xy u_{xy} + x^2 u_{yy} = 0$.
On the boundary $x = 1$, the displacement is prescribed by
$u(1,y) = e^{-|y|}$,
and $u(x,y) \to 0$ as $x \to +\infty$ uniformly in $y$.
Find the value of $u(2,0)$.
Report a single decimal number rounded to four significant digits.


In the punctured plane $r > 0$, the temperature $u(x, y)$ satisfies $x^2 u_{xx} + 2xy u_{xy} + y^2 u_{yy} = 0$. On the unit circle $r = 1$ (i.e., $(x, y) = (\cos \theta, \sin \theta)$), the boundary data are $u(\cos \theta, \sin \theta) = \sin(2\theta)$, $\left( r u_r \right) \big|_{r=1} = 1 + \cos(2\theta)$. Compute $u(1, \sqrt{3})$ and report the result as a decimal to four places.
